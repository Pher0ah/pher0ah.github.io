About BigTalk

BigTalk is a deliberately simple party game: flip a card, read the question aloud, and let everyone answer for real. Then flip it back and pass it on — the next person flips for a fresh question. No screens to stare at, no scores to keep. Just better conversations.

The idea grew out of the wonderful Make Big Talk project, which champions skipping shallow small talk in favour of questions that actually mean something. This app takes that spirit but isn't limited to it: the same card mechanic happily hosts other decks — cheeky Bad People "who's most likely" rounds, daring Bad Choices, and intimate Couples prompts — with room to add your own decks over time.

Everything runs entirely in your browser. Once loaded it works offline and can be installed to your home screen like a native app. No accounts, no tracking, no data ever leaves your device.
